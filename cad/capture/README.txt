Structure Sensor Case Design Starter Kit for iPhone 6 & iPhone 6+

This package includes some geometry and documents to help get you started with
your iPhone 6 & iPhone 6+ Structure Sensor case designs:

-CAD files (.stp, .stl, .igs):
	-iPhone 6
	-iPhone 6 Plus
	-Starter iPhone 6 Bracket
	-Starter iPhone 6 Plus Bracket
	-Structure Sensor
-Drawings (pdf):
	-Structure Sensor Mounting Hole Profile
	-Structure Sensor Recommended Alignment
